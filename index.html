A William & Mary Global Research Institute Research Lab

We conduct empircal research to inform evidence-based decision making, Buidl social impact blockchain solutions and partner with industry to solve global challenges.

About Us

In Spring of 2018 a group alumni, students and faculty came together to discuss the interest in a Blockchain Lab to unify efforts across William & Mary

In Feb. 2020, after several iterations from great feedback from a diverse group of interested parties, the initial W&M Blockchain Lab proposal received funding from W&M. The collaboration between the Global Research Institute and Entreneurship Center provided the necessary additional resources to make the W&M Blockchain Lab a reality.

WE BELIEVE IN RESEARCH, LEARNING, BUIDLING AND COMMUNITY

RESEARCH: We conduct empirical research to support evidence-based decision making at a policy and product level. We cover topics related to: Supply Chain, Trade Policy, Nuclear Proliferation,International Development, Regulation and Finance.

LEARNING: We are building learning opportunities in the classroom and beyond. Classroom education will expose students to fundamental concepts of blockchain, hands on programming opportunities and applied research techniques for evaluating blockchain applications in development context. Seminars, hackathons, computer lab, capstones and clinics are all a part of the learning experience.

BUIDLING: We are providing a place for alumni, faculty, students and the larger community to come together to buidl social impact solutions for tomorrow. Through a fantastic parnership with W&M Entrepreneurship Center, we are able to provide resources and incubation to promising projects.
